## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a chapter from the perspective of Joss Thornton, the District Officer, revealing his own constrained life and views on 'progress'.
- Create a folk tale or parable told by an elder in the community that mirrors Samuel's struggle with the land and the state.
- Draft a series of letters between Samuel and his late father, found after the story's end, that deepen their philosophical conflict.
- Write a scene set five years after the novel's end, showing the state of Parcel 17 and who, if anyone, is living there.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive map of Parcel 17, allowing users to explore Samuel's planned vs. actual land use, with soil data and crop yields.
- Create a 'Constraint Calculator' that models the trade-offs Samuel faces between bureaucratic targets, ecological limits, and personal well-being.
